## Presentation Recommendations

1. **Label each screenshot** with a descriptive title and date
2. **Group by category**: Innovation, AI Capabilities, Privacy Compliance, Technical Architecture, Deployment
3. **Include brief annotations** explaining significance for reviewers who may not be technical
4. **Use high-resolution captures** at full screen width for clarity
5. **Create a PDF portfolio** combining screenshots with brief descriptions for easy review
6. **Cross-reference** screenshots with sections of the whitepaper and impact narrative
